## Expected Challenges

### Resolution Limits
- Google Earth typically ~1m resolution in NC mountains
- 3-6 foot trails = 1-2 pixels wide (marginal detection)
- May need to scan entire trail length for visible segments

### Canopy Interference
- Deciduous forest with 50-80% coverage
- Trail may be partially obscured
- Look for canopy gaps

### Image Dating
- Satellite images may be several years old
- Trail conditions may have changed
- Use historical imagery tool if available
